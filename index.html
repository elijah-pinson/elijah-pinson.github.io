2020

In this project we created an expandable framework for allowing infinite walking in virtual reality in a closed play area by measuring saccades and redirect the user to stay within the area. A saccade is a rapid eye movement with a unique property: the eye temporarily gathers reduced information -- saccadic suppression. We leverage the suppression to redirect the user’s walking towards the center of the play area by rotating the virtual world around the camera’s location.
